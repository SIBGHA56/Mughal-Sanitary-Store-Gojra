Our Story

Where Premium
Meets Practicality

Mughal Sanitary Store was founded with a single vision: to bring world-class sanitary solutions to the people of Gojra and surrounding areas at prices that make luxury accessible. Nestled in the heart of Anarkali Bazar, our showroom has become a landmark destination for quality bathroom and kitchen fittings.

Under the expert leadership of Mehmood Mughal and Talha Mughal, we have built a reputation for excellence, offering everything from premium one-piece commodes and elegant pedestal basins to the latest LED mirrors and modern vanity units.

We stock leading brands including Star Asia Ceramics, Master, Ideal, and many more — ensuring every customer finds the perfect match for their home, office, or commercial project.
